Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Everything you need to know about booking, tours, and travel in Uganda.

You can book directly through our Services page by selecting your desired tour package, accommodation, or transport option and proceeding to the checkout. A confirmation email will be sent once payment is complete.

For immediate assistance, you can call the emergency contact number provided in your booking confirmation. For non-urgent inquiries, please use the "Get in touch" form on this page, and our support team will respond as quickly as possible.

We accept major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard) and mobile money transfer options available in Uganda. Full payment is generally required to secure your booking, but check the specific tour's policy for details.
